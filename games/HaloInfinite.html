Defeat the Banished

Set after the events of Halo 5 and shortly following a crushing defeat, the UNSC fleet lies shattered and stranded on the surface of the heavily damaged Installation 07. Amongst the wreckage, a terrifying clan of Brute warriors, The Banished, now roam the damaged ringworld. Outmanned and outgunned, Master Chief must stop the Banished from repairing the Halo ring and claiming control of the galaxy’s greatest threat.

Network Campaign Co-Op and Mission Replay

Campaign Network Co-Op allows you and up to three friends to earn 14 new Achievements and take the fight to the Banished to experience the wonder of Zeta Halo, together across Xbox and PC. With Mission Replay, you can relive your favorite moments, rediscover collectibles and explore the wonders of Zeta Halo on your own or with up to three other fireteam members!
